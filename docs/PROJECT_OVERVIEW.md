# Project Overview

## What OCIOS Is

OCIOS, or Options Chain Intelligence Operating System, is an independent applied AI and market-intelligence research prototype.

It explores how a desktop research cockpit can combine:

- live market data
- options-chain workflows
- macro regime monitoring
- model/scenario scoring
- portfolio context
- diagnostics
- AI-assisted explanation
- human decision support

---

## What OCIOS Is Not

OCIOS is not:

- financial advice
- investment advice
- a live automated trading platform
- a production brokerage system
- a commercial SaaS product
- a guarantee of profitable trades

The project is positioned as a research and decision-support prototype.

---

## Why It Matters

Many complex workflows fail because information is fragmented across separate tools, logs, dashboards, files, models, and APIs.

OCIOS explores a more operational approach:

```text
collect the data
normalize the data
score the situation
explain the result
show the system status
keep the human in control
```

---

## Key Research Questions

OCIOS explores questions such as:

- How can options-chain data be made more reviewable?
- How can model status be made visible instead of hidden?
- How can weak or scenario-only candidates be labeled honestly?
- How can macro regime context be integrated into research workflows?
- How can diagnostics help the user understand why data or predictions are missing?
- How can a dashboard separate research recommendations from execution?
